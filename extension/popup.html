Before you start

Your research stays under your control.

findEmm processes the page details you choose to capture plus prospect contact data you enter or retrieve from permitted sources. It stores saved records locally in an encrypted vault and sends only your requested lookup data to your configured local API or authorized provider.

No data is sold, used for ads, or sent to a findEmm-operated cloud service.
